Points are awarded based upon number of entries in each pool.  First place finishers get points equal to 65% of the number of players in the pool, second place gets 25% and third place gets 10%.  For the weekly winners in the NFL Regular Season Pick 'Em Pool, each weekly winner gets points equal to 1/17th of the number of players in the pool, since there are 17 weeks to the season.  For the winner of individual Golf tournaments, each winner gets points equal to 1/4 of the number of players in the pool.

The column "Other" refers to the NFL weekly winners and the Golf individual tournament winners.

Grayed out name = has not been active in a Waldarry Pool for over two years

Pool years are based on the year in which the season ends and the champion is determined
