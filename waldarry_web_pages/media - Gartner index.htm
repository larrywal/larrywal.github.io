Gartner Software & Service Development Group Video - 1999 (15:47) - motivational video

This video was created for the GS2 annual meeting in Maitland, Florida on October 15, 1999.  GS2 (Gartner Software & Service Development) was the forerunner to the ITG organization.  The video was created by Billy, Bob and Larry Waldman (Billy did the impressive up-front graphics), and was the first in a series of four very successful annual meeting videos.

Interactive Technology Group Video - 2002 (23:27) - motivational video

The ITG organization came to an end in late 2002, and this video was dedicated to the four years of GS2 and ITG evolution.  It incorporated clips of the first three videos (1999, 2000 and 2001) and then added a final look back at 2002.
