Waldarry Festival 2006

Another great Waldarry Festival took place on July 15, 2006, celebrating 30 years on Brookdale Drive.  There were 123 attendees for the day long event, which featured kayaking trips on Waldarry Pond and Poorhouse Brook.  Lots of kids (three under one year old), and even folks in their eighties and nineties!  Great fun - so check out the pictures - maybe you're in there!
