Waldarry Festival 2010

Pam finally joined Bob in retirement, and that was the theme for this year's Festival.  It was a brutally hot day with full sun and well into the 90's, but 187 people still braved it out, and over 100 were still there for the fantastic ("best ever") fireworks display.  If you'd like to see the Grand Finale again, or if you just happened not to be there, you can see the video (4:34) on YouTube at Festival Fireworks 2010.

You can see a collection of photos from the day at Festival Photos 2010.

Thanks to all who came and helped make it a great day!
