Waldarry Festival 2007

Another great Waldarry Festival took place on July 14, 2007, celebrating the first anniversary of the Waldarry Wildlife Preserve.  There were 173 attendees for the day long event.  There were folks of all ages, and a new highlight this year was the fire-pit, for roasting marshmallows and s'mores while awaiting the spectacular fireworks.  See if you can find yourself in the Festival Photos!  (special thanks to Mark, Enrique, Ralph & Nora for contributing photos.)
