FLORENCE (FIRENZE)

We left Rome and drove through Perugia to get to Florence. We arrived on a Sunday and were able to park downtown. We briefly visited the Piazza del Duomo, Campanile, Piazza della Signoria, Palazzo Vecchio, the beautiful bronze doors, Ponte Vecchio, etc. Again we walked and walked. We even went into the Bargello Palace and Museum to see Michelangelo’s statue of David. The architectural styles were fascinating.
