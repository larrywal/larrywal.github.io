PISA & PORTOVENERE

Pisa is just how it’s pictured in the magazines. However, during the last few years the views have become somewhat less picturesque as construction crews have used concrete slabs and blocks, along with multiple cables, to keep the tower from falling.

Portovenere is a beautiful little city on the Italian Riviera. As Americans, we are used to bigger beaches, but having the hotel’s salt water pool overlook the bay was lovely. Portovenere is a severe looking town (the colorful scenery almost looks fake) dating back as far as the 12C. Most evenings, we took leisurely strolls along the shore and ate overlooking the water watching the many yachts.
