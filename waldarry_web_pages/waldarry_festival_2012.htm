Waldarry Festival 2012

The theme this year was our first use of a Quick Response Code on our invitation.  Forty-four attendees were geeky enough to use the code to learn our secret password and earn themselves an original Waldarry Festival drink Koozie.  Wow!

After two straight years of scorching hot Festivals, we had a beautiful day with temperatures in the high seventies and low eighties.  We had 110 comfortable guests in attendance (except for the two who fell into the pond!).

You can see a collection of photos from the day at Festival Photos 2012.  In addition, the complete fireworks video (17 minutes) can be seen at Festival Fireworks 2012.  This was our very first fully electronics fireworks show - the culmination of two years of development!  Thanks to all who came and helped make it a great day!
