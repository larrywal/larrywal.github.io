Waldarry Festival 2009

 

Festival 2009 celebrated 35 years - Festival XXXV!  It was a great sunny day with 199 attendees, the most since the wedding of '05.  A record 26 guests were still there the next morning - wow!

If you did not stay for the fireworks - or even if you did - you can view the Grand Finale (4:36) on YouTube.  You can also click to see all the pictures from the great day!
