After working for Lockheed Martin from 2005 to 2010, and then a spin-off called the SI Organization (now known as Vencore) until 2013, I left for San Francisco and a position with the Meraki Division of Cisco.  In 2015 I joined a start-up company, Samsara, also in San Francisco, where I am still plugging away..

Work keeps me very busy, but I do find time for a host of other activities, especially in the world of sports.  I play some ice hockey and volleyball, and really like spending vacation time kite-surfing. 

I visit Connecticut a few times a year and enjoy seeing my twin brother Larry, as well as Dad and  Pam and the rest of the Waldarry gang.  I also really enjoy participating in the Waldarry Sports Pools competitions.  I hold fourth place on the all-time Top Ten and hope to improve upon that in the coming year.
