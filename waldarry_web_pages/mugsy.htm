Hello.  I'm Mugsy and I am now about twelve years old.  I live with my Daddy (BJ) and and Mommy (Amanda) and I do everything I can to make them happy.  I used to be a wild dog, jumping on everybody, whining a lot, and being a general pain.  But now I have settled down and have become a really (or perhaps somewhat) good dog.  At least I think so!

I sometimes go over to Pam & Bob's and it's fun to run around there backyard with my sister Brownie.  In quieter times I like to sit on Bob's lap and he sings doowop songs to me.
