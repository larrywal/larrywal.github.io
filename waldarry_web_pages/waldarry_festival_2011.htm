Waldarry Festival 2011

It was a stretch this year, but the theme was 5,000 hot dogs served.  We've been at it since 1976!   The weather was a repeat of last year - a high of 93 degrees keeping us all warm and toasty.  We still had 131 people making an appearance , and most still there for the fantastic fireworks display.  If you'd like to see the Grand Finale again, or if you just happened not to be there, you can see the video (5:16) on YouTube at Festival Fireworks 2011.

You can see a collection of photos from the day at Festival Photos 2011.

Thanks to all who came and helped make it a great day!
